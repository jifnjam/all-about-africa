About Us

All About Africa is designed to be an educational website solely about Africa, dispelling stereotypes and opening up a different kind of worldview. The information stems from reputed news organizations, government websites and websites representing different minority groups and regions. This is not intended to be used inappropriately and is solely for self-education from kids to adults.
