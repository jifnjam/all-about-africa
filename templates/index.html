Often Misunderstood and Stereotyped.

Africa's population of over 1.4 Billion is split up into over 5 different regions, covering over 20% of the Earth's landmass and housing over 3,000 languages. Each of the 54 countries of Africa have delicious food, storied culture, stunning architecture, and scenic geography.

Despite this, people may have preconceived stereotypes about Africa such as Africa being filled with poor, diseased, and uneducated people. That Africans speak "African", cities aren't present in Africa, internet isn't common, and that a desert covers the country.

These are quite negative and don't properly highlight ALL of the aspects of Africa and its peoples. All About Africa intends to tell its visitors about Africa without the presence of these stereotypes and highlight the positive influences made by Africa all over the world.
