About Us

Hello, I'm Tobi! I manage allaboutafrica.com. I started this website with the goal of making the awesome things about Africa awesome to everyone else! Hopefully, the longer you spend here, the more you'll get to appreciate the world around you. Here there is information on each country's geography, climate, cuisine, politics, history, demographics, and some fun facts. All of the information on allaboutafrica.com is intended to be wholly factual and work towards eliminating stereotypes about the countries.
